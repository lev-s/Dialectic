dialectic

For all types of diabetes, type 1, type 2, or gestational diabetes, understanding how food, physical activity, and your blood sugar levels interact is critical for controlling your condition. It can be overwhelming to think about carb counts, insulin doses, A1C, glucose, glycemic index, blood pressure, weight… the list goes on! But with the help of Dialectic you can simplify tracking. Use them to consolidate your health information into one spot so you can make informed choices to manage your health.

Dialectic seeks to maintain a fully operational user-interface that would be friendly to use and understand the manticism of personal insulin tracking, which will help to lane of insulin dosages, blood glucose levels -hypoglycemia, normoglycemia, and hyperglycemia- and carbohydrate/calorie count for one meal.

When it comes to managing diabetes, knowledge is power. Dialectic is here to support you in figuring out how to organize your blood glucose levels and track you insulin dosages.

Tracking your sugar levels – either with a blood glucose monitor (BGM) or a CGM – allows you to make decisions about any adjustment to your insulin dosing straightaway. The data gathered over a period of time also allows you and your doctor or nurse to assess trends, triggers and the amount of time you spend in or outside of your glycaemic targets.
